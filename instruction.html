Creating An Account

It's Easy enough.
To Create an account for your self, goto the homepage https://speedmaths.edu.lk And Select The Sign-Up Button. Fill out with necessary Details and submit your application.

Activating An Account

In order to activate an account, you have to make the payment of Rs.500/=for 1 month or Rs.4800/=for 1 year ( According To Your Requirement ). And send the receipt to speedmathslk@gmail.com or Whatsapp to 0724874919. Within 24 Hours after the payment, your Account will be activated. For payment details got the Payment Section Here.

Changing Account Settings

To change your Account Settings, First Log-In to your account, And Select Account Settings Button From the navigation Menu.

If you are on a mobile Device, First Select the hamburger menu and then select account settings.

Attending Training

After Activating your account, You can attend for training.
First Log-In To your account and go to the Training Section from the navigation menu, or scroll down.



From here, you can select exercise to your heart's content. Adjust levels to your upper limits and you'll be improving in no time.

Resetting Password

If you forgot your current password, you can get a reset link from Password Resetting Section. Enter your email and you'll get an resetting link to you email. Follow the instructions there to reset your password.
